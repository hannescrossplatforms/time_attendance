Frequently Asked Questions
  • Q) How much bandwidth can I use at a HipZone™ ?
  • A) You can use 50Mb per day for FREE at any HipZone™ Wi-Fi venue (see below)

  • Q) What do I need to do to get 50MB per day
  • A) Carry on enjoying our service is the simple answer. In addition your input will be requested in the form of 2 simple questions each time you login. Some of which will ensure your voice is heard and others will provide our brand sponsors the insights they need to shape their products and services for you.

  • Q) How come my 50MB just vanishes?
  • A) Both Apple and Windows based operating systems have automatic system updates turned ON by default, and sometimes Anti-virus updates can suck up your data allowance. We suggest that you turn OFF the automatic system updates and manually update your system when on your home network. If you need help please call 0861 GETHIP (438447).

  • Q) I can't send mail, what do I do?
  • A) Change your SMTP server in your email settings to smtp.hipzone.net. If you need help, please call 0861 GET HIP (0861 438 447).

  • Q) What sort of questions?
  • A)Initially the questions will be about you, your internet habits and lifestyle. Then, there will be all sorts of questions about all sorts of topics.

  • Q)What do you do with the information you collect?
  • A) We provide insights to our advertising partners about the habits of the HipZone™ internet community.

  • Q) Do you pass on personal information like my email address to all your advertisers?
  • A) NO. NEVER. HipZone™ is fully compliant with The Promotion of Access to Information Act 2 of 2000 (PAIA). Your email address is kept secure at all times. The only information that would be passed on is incidental, for example your age and gender, and that is grouped with all other respondents.

  • Q) Will I get bombarded with emails now?
  • A) No. HipZone™ will only send you one email per month.
